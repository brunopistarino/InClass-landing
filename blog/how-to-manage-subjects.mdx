title: How to manage subjects
description: Learn how to add, edit and remove a subject
---

### Add or modify a subject

1. Tap the **Settings** button on the navigation.
2. Go to **Subjects** in the **Data** section.

![add-edit-a-subject](/blog/iphone-settings-subjects-tutorial.png)

3. Tap the **+ button** on the navigation bar to add a subject or tap the subject you want to edit.

![add-edit-a-subject](/blog/iphone-subjects-tutorial.png)

4. Fill in or modify the form.

> Note: the **Name** field is required.

5. Tap the **Add time** button to add a time or slide to delete.
6. Click on the **Save** button.
7. The subject will be added to the list.

![add-edit-a-subject](/blog/iphone-modify-subject-tutorial.png)

### Delete a subject

Slide a subject from right to left to show the **Delete** button.

![add-edit-a-subject](/blog/iphone-delete-subject.png)
